美国国务院的“正义的奖赏”（RFJ）项目现给予可达1000万美元的奖励，对能导致识别和定位以下人士的信息进行奖励：任何与某一外国政府共事或为某一外国政府工作，通过非法网络活动干扰美国选举的人士。


更多有关这项奖励的信息，访问“正义的奖赏”网站 https://rewardsforjustice.net/chinese 和阅读这份声明：https://china.usembassy-china.org.cn/zh/reward-offer-for-information-on-foreign-interference-in-u-s-elections-zh/



The U.S. Department of State’s Rewards for Justice (RFJ) program is offering a reward of up to $10 million for information leading to the identification or location of any person who works with or for a foreign government for the purpose of interfering with U.S. elections through certain illegal cyber activities. 


More information about this reward offer is located on the Rewards for Justice website at https://rewardsforjustice.net/chinese and in this statement: https://china.usembassy-china.org.cn/reward-offer-for-information-on-foreign-interference-in-u-s-elections/
